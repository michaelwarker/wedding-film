IV. Hi, I'm Michael.

After documenting over 100 weddings, I've learned not only how to make a great film and photo album, but I've also learned how to be a great partner to my couples' on their special day. I pride myself when the backup bowtie I've packed gets used by the father of the groom, or when the dress tape I pack helps a bridesmaid getting in her dress, or the couple feels so comfortable to cry next to me, and I have a pack of tissues waiting for them to use.

For me, documenting weddings isn't just a job. It's a real privilege for me to create a piece of art with you that could only exist from the magic we make together. I want to build something that you'll look back on every year on your anniversary, cuddling with your partner, and laughing at all the special moments from the best day of your lives.
