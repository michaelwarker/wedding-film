Booth Packages

Many photo booths capture low quality images meant for tiny print-outs, but this booth is different. I use the same gear I would photograph a wedding with to capture high quality photographs that are delivered to you as a separate wedding gallery, documenting all of the best moments of you with your family and friends all night long. I offer not only standard prints on the day, but also custom stickers made from the photographs of your guests, ready to take home so they can remember your wedding for years to come.

For weddings and events that want a quick and easy set-up without physical keepsakes. Digital photos are delivered straight to guests and full galleries are delivered to you following your event.

  • Unlimited digital photos
  • Instant text, email, or QR code sharing
  • Online gallery delivery after the event
  • Up to four hours of coverage
