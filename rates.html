Video Packages

All weddings are recorded with multiple tripod set-ups during your ceremony and toasts for real-time coverage. Audio is captured through a mix of Lavalier Microphones and audio output from your venue or DJ's audio system.

For couples on a budget who just want the most important moments of the day captured. This option often sacrifices guest and wedding party coverage in favor of telling your story.

  • 1 videographer
  • 6 hours of coverage
  • 1-minute highlight film (social-ready)
  • Raw footage included

This option allows time for full coverage from a singular shooter, telling a more complete story of the day.

  • 1 videographer
  • 8 hours of coverage
  • 3–5 minute highlight film
  • Ceremony + toasts edit
  • 1-minute social recap
  • Raw footage included
  • Drone coverage included

Introducing a second shooter into the day allows for a substantial increase in coverage, allowing for each shooter to give their attention to both halves of the couple. The second shooter gives more options for artful shots of the wedding ceremony and toasts, along with allowing more time for me to film artistic shots of the venue from the ground or from the air.

  • 2 videographers
  • 8 hours of coverage
  • 5-minute highlight film
  • Ceremony + toasts edit
  • 1-minute social recap
  • Drone coverage included
  • Raw footage included

This is a premium for couples wanting every second of their special day captured. It also allows for an additional portrait session whenever availability allows, giving us plenty of time outside of a busy day to film beautiful shots of the two of you in the location of your choice.

  • 2 videographers
  • 10 hours of coverage
  • 5-minute highlight film + 30-minute feature edit
  • Ceremony + toasts edit
  • 1-minute social recap
  • Drone coverage included
  • Engagement film session or additional couples' portrait session included

Photo Packages

Photography packages can be bundled with video for a discount. If you book both, I'll hire a trusted photographer from the many I've worked with to bring your day to life.

For couples on a budget who just want the most important moments of the day captured. Inevitably, these photographs focus more on your details, portraits, and the ceremony, losing out on some candid moments getting ready and on the dance floor at the end of the night.

  • 1 photographer
  • 300 edited photos
  • Online gallery

This option allows for a more complete gallery, documenting your day from getting ready to partying at the end of the night. It also includes a separate engagement or other portrait session, allowing plenty of time for artistic posed shots.

  • 1 photographer
  • 8 hours of coverage
  • 400 edited photos
  • Online gallery
  • Engagement session or additional couples' portrait session included

Introducing a second photographer dramatically increases the amount that can be covered. The second photographer allows for both halves of the couple to have a photographer solely devoted to them. They also allow the lead photographer to capture more risky, artistic shots while they cover the essentials, making for a more well-rounded gallery. In addition, their presence allows for more guest coverage, showing you how much your close friends and family enjoyed your special day. Finally, to be truly cinematic, the flexibility of the second photographer gives the lead time to capture stunning aerial photography showcasing your venue.

  • 2 photographers
  • 8 hours of coverage
  • 600 edited photos
  • Online gallery
  • Engagement session or additional couples' portrait session included
  • Drone photography included

This package includes everything, making it the most premium photography offering. This is for the couples that want to remember every moment of their special day. The extra hours allow us breathing room to make sure every family photo, every combination of friends, and of course every portrait of both of you is captured to your heart's content.

  • 2 photographers
  • 10 hours of coverage
  • 800+ edited photos
  • Online gallery
  • Engagement session or additional couples' portrait session included
  • Rehearsal Dinner coverage or 2 additional hours of coverage on the Wedding Day from lead photographer
